About

What is VibeAI?

VibeAI is founded by Gbadegesin Alawode. It is a trusted, secured and AI‑powered online platform that delivers accurate and culturally sensitive HIV/SRHR information to adolescents and young people. VibeAI also helps simplify and aggregate data insights and relevant publications for policymakers, researchers and donors.

Secure by design
Privacy‑minded architecture and responsible AI guardrails.
Human + AI
Curated content reviewed by experts, delivered with scalable AI.
Context‑aware
Culturally sensitive responses routed to local services where available.

Best Practices

From strategy to scalable action

Youth co‑design

Co‑create content & flows with adolescents to ensure relevance and dignity.

Safety by default

Age‑appropriate responses; crisis keywords trigger escalation & human review.

Data minimalism

Collect only what’s necessary; transparent consent and retention policies.

Facility linkages

Bi‑directional referral loops between community and health facilities.

Evidence first

Rapid learning cycles; outcomes tracked with clear indicators.

Localization

Language and cultural nuance prioritized for inclusivity.

Implementation checklist

  • Define safeguarding & escalation pathways
  • Map services & youth‑friendly facilities
  • Design content taxonomy & quality review cadence
  • Set up analytics with privacy guards
  • Plan iterative pilots with clear success criteria

Governance & trust

Adopt transparent model cards, publish updates and invite independent audits where appropriate.
